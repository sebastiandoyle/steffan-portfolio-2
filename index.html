About

Steffan Doyle is an Australian comedy creator and actor who has built one of TikTok's most consistently viral comedy presences. With 134 million views across his content library, Steffan's character impressions — particularly his Rio movie bird series — have captured massive audiences. His wedding lineup content alone generated over 36 million views across the series.

Beyond TikTok, Steffan holds acting credits including 'The Spider' on IMDb. His ability to create authentic, shareable comedy content makes him an ideal partner for brands targeting Gen-Z audiences who value genuine entertainment over polished advertising.
